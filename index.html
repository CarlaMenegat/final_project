About the project

Cleopatra is a project developed within the framework of the Knowledge Organization in Libraries and Archives course of the Digital Humanities and Digital Knowledge Master's program at the University of Bologna.
This project focuses on Cleopatra VII Thea Philopator, the famous queen of Ancient Egypt.

Scenario

In the realm of historical figures, few captivate the imagination quite like Cleopatra VII Philopator, the last active ruler of the Ptolemaic Kingdom of Egypt. Renowned for her intellect, charisma, and political prowess, Cleopatra's legacy has endured through centuries, inspiring artists, scholars, and historians alike. In this scenario, we embark on a journey through a Linked Open Data (LOD) project centered around Cleopatra, utilizing interconnected datasets to illuminate various facets of her life, reign, and impact on history.
